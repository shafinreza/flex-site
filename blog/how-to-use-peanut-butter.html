How to Use Peanut Butter for Performance

Published March 2026 • 5 min read

Peanut butter is one of the most underrated performance foods. When used correctly, it provides sustained energy, healthy fats, and protein that supports training and recovery.

1. Pre-Workout Fuel

A tablespoon of natural peanut butter 60–90 minutes before training provides slow-release energy without sugar spikes. Pair it with a banana or oats for optimal carbohydrate balance.

2. Post-Workout Recovery

Combine peanut butter with oats or wholegrain toast to replenish glycogen and support muscle repair through quality fats and protein.

3. Daily Nutrition

High-quality peanut butter with no palm oil or added sugar can be a clean staple in any balanced diet. Choose minimally processed options made from 100% roasted peanuts.
