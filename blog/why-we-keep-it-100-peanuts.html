Why We Keep It 100% Peanuts

Published March 2026 • 4 min read

Most supermarket peanut butters contain more than just peanuts. Palm oil, added sugar, stabilisers and fillers are common. At FLEX, we keep it simple: 100% roasted peanuts.

No Palm Oil

Palm oil is often added to prevent separation and improve texture. But clean peanut butter doesn’t need it. Natural separation is a sign of minimal processing — just stir and enjoy.

No Added Sugar

Peanuts naturally contain flavour. Adding sugar increases calories without improving performance. We believe your energy should come from quality fats and protein, not sweeteners.

No Fillers

Fillers dilute nutritional value. A true performance product should be concentrated, simple and honest.

One ingredient. Maximum focus. That’s FLEX.
